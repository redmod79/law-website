    Raw Concept Context Output¶

    Concept Context: 2 Corinthians 3:7¶

    Verse: "But if the ministration of death, written [and] engraven in stones, was glorious, so that the children of Israel could not stedfastly behold the face of Moses for the glory of his countenance; which [glory] was to be done away:"

    Concepts Identified: - DEATH: G2288 (thanatos) - GLORY: G1391 (doxa)

    Same Chapter (2 Corinthians 3) -- 5 verses with GLORY concept:¶

    1. 2 Cor 3:10 -- "that which was made glorious had no glory...by reason of the glory that excelleth"
    2. 2 Cor 3:11 -- "if that which is done away [was] glorious, much more that which remaineth [is] glorious"
    3. 2 Cor 3:18 -- "beholding...the glory of the Lord, are changed...from glory to glory"
    4. 2 Cor 3:8 -- "the ministration of the spirit be rather glorious"
    5. 2 Cor 3:9 -- "the ministration of condemnation [be] glory...the ministration of righteousness exceed in glory"

    Same Book (2 Corinthians) -- Key GLORY + DEATH verses:¶

    • 2 Cor 4:6 -- "the light of the knowledge of the glory of God in the face of Jesus Christ"
    • 2 Cor 4:17 -- "our light affliction...worketh for us...eternal weight of glory"

    Same Author (Paul) -- Key cross-references:¶

    • Romans 6:4 -- "raised up from the dead by the glory of the Father" (DEATH + GLORY)
    • 1 Cor 15:43 -- "sown in dishonour; raised in glory"
    • 1 Cor 15:56 -- "The sting of death is sin; the strength of sin is the law"

    Concept Context: 2 Corinthians 3:3¶

    Verse: "[Forasmuch as ye are] manifestly declared to be the epistle of Christ ministered by us, written not with ink, but with the Spirit of the living God; not in tables of stone, but in fleshy tables of the heart."

    Concepts Identified: - MESSIAH: G5547 (Christos) - SPIRIT: G4151 (pneuma) - LIFE: G2198 (zoe/zao) - HEART: G2588 (kardia)

    Same Chapter (2 Corinthians 3) -- 8 verses:¶

    1. 2 Cor 3:14 [MESSIAH] -- "which [vail] is done away in Christ"
    2. 2 Cor 3:15 [HEART] -- "the vail is upon their heart"
    3. 2 Cor 3:17 [SPIRIT] -- "the Lord is that Spirit"
    4. 2 Cor 3:18 [SPIRIT] -- "by the Spirit of the Lord"
    5. 2 Cor 3:2 [HEART] -- "written in our hearts"
    6. 2 Cor 3:6 [SPIRIT] -- "not of the letter, but of the spirit"

    Same Author (Paul) -- Key verses with multiple concepts:¶

    • Romans 8:2 [LIFE, MESSIAH, SPIRIT] -- "the law of the Spirit of life in Christ Jesus"
    • Romans 8:10 [LIFE, MESSIAH, SPIRIT] -- "if Christ be in you...the Spirit is life"
    • Colossians 3:16 [HEART, MESSIAH, SPIRIT] -- "the word of Christ dwell in you...singing with grace in your hearts"
    • 1 Thess 5:23 [LIFE, MESSIAH, SPIRIT] -- "your whole spirit and soul and body be preserved...unto the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ"

    Concept Context: 2 Corinthians 3:14¶

    Verse: "But their minds were blinded: for until this day remaineth the same vail untaken away in the reading of the old testament; which [vail] is done away in Christ."

    Concepts Identified: - COVENANT: G1242 (diatheke) - MESSIAH: G5547 (Christos)

    Same Chapter (2 Corinthians 3) -- 3 verses:¶

    1. 2 Cor 3:3 [MESSIAH] -- "the epistle of Christ"
    2. 2 Cor 3:4 [MESSIAH] -- "through Christ to God-ward"
    3. 2 Cor 3:6 [COVENANT] -- "ministers of the new testament (diatheke)"

    Same Author (Paul) -- Key COVENANT + MESSIAH:¶

    • Ephesians 2:12 -- "without Christ...strangers from the covenants of promise"
    • Galatians 3:17 -- "the covenant confirmed before of God in Christ...the law...cannot disannul"

    Concept Context: 2 Corinthians 3:18¶

    Verse: "But we all, with open face beholding as in a glass the glory of the Lord, are changed into the same image from glory to glory, [even] as by the Spirit of the Lord."

    Concepts Identified: - GLORY: G1391 (doxa) -- appears 3x in this verse - SPIRIT: G4151 (pneuma)

    Same Chapter (2 Corinthians 3) -- 8 verses:¶

    1. 2 Cor 3:8 [GLORY, SPIRIT] -- "the ministration of the spirit be rather glorious"
    2. 2 Cor 3:7 [GLORY] -- "the glory of his countenance"
    3. 2 Cor 3:9 [GLORY] -- "the ministration of righteousness exceed in glory"
    4. 2 Cor 3:10 [GLORY] -- "the glory that excelleth"
    5. 2 Cor 3:11 [GLORY] -- "that which is done away [was] glorious...that which remaineth [is] glorious"

    Same Author (Paul) -- Key GLORY + SPIRIT:¶

    • 1 Cor 6:20 -- "glorify God in your body, and in your spirit"
    • 1 Tim 3:16 -- "justified in the Spirit...received up into glory"
    • Ephesians 1:17 -- "the Father of glory, may give unto you the spirit of wisdom"
    • Ephesians 3:16 -- "according to the riches of his glory, to be strengthened...by his Spirit"

    Other Books -- Key GLORY + SPIRIT:¶

    • Acts 7:55 -- "full of the Holy Ghost, looked up stedfastly into heaven, and saw the glory of God"
    • 1 Peter 4:14 -- "the spirit of glory and of God resteth upon you"
    • 1 Peter 1:11 -- "the Spirit of Christ...testified...the sufferings of Christ, and the glory that should follow"
