    H8451: torah (תּוֹרָה)¶

    • Transliteration: towrah
    • Pronunciation: to-raw
    • Part of Speech: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 219
    • Definition: from yarah (H3384); a precept or statute, especially the Decalogue or Pentateuch: -- law.
    • Total KJV occurrences: 244
    • Primary translations: "the law" (60x, 24.6%), "of the law" (28x, 11.5%), "law" (24x, 9.8%), "thy law" (20x, 8.2%), "in the law" (19x, 7.8%), "my law" (10x, 4.1%)

    G3551: nomos (νόμος)¶

    • Transliteration: nomos
    • Pronunciation: nom-os
    • Part of Speech: masculine noun
    • BLB Count: 197
    • Definition: from nemo (to parcel out, especially food or grazing to animals); anything established, anything received by usage, a custom, a law, a command
    • Total KJV occurrences: 169
    • Primary translations: "law" (95x, 56.2%), "the law" (49x, 29.0%), "of the law" (14x, 8.3%)
    • Key NT uses (complete): Mat 5:17, 5:18, 7:12, 11:13, 12:5, 15:6, 22:36, 22:40, Luk 2:22-39, 10:26, 16:17, 24:44, Jhn 1:17,45,46, 7:19,23,49,51, 8:5,17, 10:34, 12:34, 15:25, 19:7, Act 6:13, 7:53, 13:15,38,39, 15:5,24, 18:13,15, 21:20,24, 22:12, 23:3, 24:14, 28:23, Rom 2:9-27, 3:19-31, 4:11-16, 5:13-14,20, 6:14-15, 7:1-25, 8:2-4,7, 9:31, 10:4, 13:8-10, 1Co 9:8-20, 14:21, 15:56, Gal 2:19,21, 3:2-25, 4:4,21, 5:3,4,14,18,23, 6:2,13, Eph 2:15, Php 3:5, 1Ti 1:8,9, Heb 7:5,12,16,19,28, 8:10, 9:22, 10:1,16,28, Jas 1:25, 2:8,10,12

    H4687: mitsvah (מִצְוָה)¶

    • Transliteration: mitsvah
    • Pronunciation: mits-vaw
    • Part of Speech: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 181
    • Definition: from tsavah (H6680); a command, whether human or divine (collectively, the Law): -- (which was) commanded(-ment), law, ordinance, precept.
    • Total KJV occurrences: 190
    • Primary translations: "the commandments" (27x, 14.2%), "his commandments" (24x, 12.6%), "my commandments" (23x, 12.1%), "thy commandments" (20x, 10.5%), "the commandment" (14x, 7.4%)
    • Key uses: Gen 26:5, Exo 15:26, 16:28, 20:6, 24:12, Deu 4:2, 5:28, 6:1,2,17,25, 8:1,2,11, 10:13, 11:1,13, 26:13, 30:11, 31:5, 1Ki 2:3, 6:12, 8:58, 2Ki 18:6, 23:3, Neh 1:7, 9:14, Psa 19:9, 119:96, Pro 2:1, 3:1, 6:20,23, 7:1,2, 13:13, 19:16, Isa 29:13, Jer 35:14,16,18, Dan 9:5, Mal 2:1,4

    • Definition: from entellomai; injunction, i.e. an authoritative prescription -- commandment, precept
    • Total KJV occurrences: 43
    • Primary translations: "commandment" (23x, 53.5%), "commandments" (12x, 27.9%), "precept" (2x, 4.7%)
    • Key uses: Mat 15:3, 19:17, 22:36,38,40, Mrk 7:8, 10:5,19, 12:28, Luk 1:6, 18:20, 23:56, Jhn 10:18, 12:49,50, 13:34, 14:15,21,31, 15:10,12, Rom 7:8-13, 13:9, 1Co 7:19, Eph 2:15, 6:2, 1Ti 6:14, Heb 7:5,16,18, 9:19, 1Jn 2:3,7, 3:22-24, 4:21, 5:2,3, 2Jn 1:4-6, Rev 12:17, 14:12, 22:14

    H676: etsba (אֶצְבַּע)¶

    • Transliteration: etsba
    • Pronunciation: ets-bah
    • Part of Speech: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 32
    • Definition: from same as etsba (in the sense of grasping); something to seize with, i.e. a finger; by analogy, a toe -- finger, toe
    • Total KJV occurrences: 34
    • Primary translations: "with his finger" (9x, 26.5%), "his finger" (3x, 8.8%), "finger" (2x, 5.9%), "fingers" (2x, 5.9%)
    • KEY USES for this study:
    • Exo 8:15 "Then the magicians said unto Pharaoh, This is the finger of God"
    • Exo 31:18 "tables of stone, written with the finger of God"
    • Deu 9:10 "two tables of stone written with the finger of God"
    • Psa 8:4 "When I consider thy heavens, the work of thy fingers"

    H2706: choq (חֹק)¶

    • Transliteration: choq
    • Pronunciation: khoke
    • Part of Speech: masculine noun
    • BLB Count: 127
    • Definition: from chaqaq (H2710); an enactment; hence, an appointment (of time, space, quantity, labor or usage): -- appointed, bound, commandment, convenient, custom, decree(-d), due, law, measure, necessary, ordinance(-nary), portion, set time, statute, task
    • Total KJV occurrences: 134
    • Primary translations: "the statutes" (14x, 10.4%), "thy statutes" (14x, 10.4%), "statutes" (13x, 9.7%), "a statute" (7x, 5.2%), "his statutes" (7x, 5.2%)
    • Key uses: Gen 47:22, Exo 5:14, 12:24, 15:25,26, 18:16,20, Lev 10:11, 26:46, Deu 4:1,5,6,8,14,40,45, 5:1,28, 6:1,17,20,24, 7:11, 26:16,17, 27:10, Jos 24:25, 1Ki 3:14, 8:58,61, 9:4, Neh 9:13,14, Job 23:12,14, Psa 2:7, 50:16, 81:5,6, 94:20, 99:7, 105:10,45, 119:5, 147:19, 148:6, Pro 8:29, Isa 24:5, Jer 5:22, 31:36, Eze 20:18,25, 36:27, Mal 3:7

    H4941: mishpat (מִשְׁפָּט)¶

    • Transliteration: mishpat
    • Pronunciation: mish-pawt
    • Part of Speech: masculine noun
    • BLB Count: 421
    • Definition: from shaphat (H8199); properly, a verdict (favorable or unfavorable) pronounced judicially, especially a sentence or formal decree; abstractly, justice, including a participant's right or privilege (statutory or customary) -- judgment, justice, manner, right, ordinance
    • Total KJV occurrences: 448
    • Primary translations: "judgment" (69x, 15.4%), "my judgments" (23x, 5.1%), "of judgment" (21x, 4.7%)
    • Key uses: Psa 19:9 "the judgments of the LORD are true and righteous altogether", Psa 111:7 "the works of his hands are verity and judgment"

    G40: hagios (ἅγιος)¶

    • Transliteration: hagios
    • Pronunciation: hag-ee-os
    • Part of Speech: adjective
    • BLB Count: 229
    • Definition: from hagos (an awful thing); sacred (physically, pure, morally blameless or religious, ceremonially consecrated) -- (most) holy (one, thing), saint
    • Total KJV occurrences: 219
    • Primary translations: "holy" (51x, 23.3%), "saints" (47x, 21.5%), "Holy" (37x, 16.9%)
    • KEY USE: Rom 7:12 "the law is holy (hagios), and the commandment holy (hagios), and just, and good"

    G4152: pneumatikos (πνευματικός)¶

    • Transliteration: pneumatikos
    • Pronunciation: pnyoo-mat-ik-os
    • Part of Speech: adjective
    • BLB Count: 26
    • Definition: from pneuma; non-carnal, i.e. (humanly) ethereal (as opposed to gross), or (divinely) spiritual -- spiritual
    • Total KJV occurrences: 26
    • Primary translations: "spiritual" (13x, 50%), "spiritual things" (3x), "is spiritual" (3x), "a spiritual" (3x)
    • KEY USE: Rom 7:14 "we know that the law is spiritual (pneumatikos)"
    • Other key uses: 1Co 2:10-16 (spiritual understanding), 1Co 10:3,4 (spiritual meat/drink), 1Co 15:46 (spiritual body), Eph 1:3 (spiritual blessings), Eph 6:12 (spiritual wickedness)

    G1345: dikaioma (δικαίωμα)¶

    • Transliteration: dikaioma
    • Pronunciation: dik-ah-yo-mah
    • Part of Speech: neuter noun
    • BLB Count: 10
    • Definition: from dikaioo; an equitable deed; by implication, a statute or decision -- judgment, justification, ordinance, righteousness
    • Total KJV occurrences: 10
    • Primary translations: "ordinances" (3x, 30%), "righteousness" (3x, 30%), "judgment" (1x), "justification" (1x)
    • KEY USES:
    • Rom 1:32 "the judgment (dikaioma) of God"
    • Rom 2:26 "keep the righteousness (dikaioma) of the law"
    • Rom 5:16,18 "justification (dikaioma)"
    • Rom 8:4 "the righteousness (dikaioma) of the law might be fulfilled in us" -- CRITICAL for this study
    • Heb 9:1,10 "ordinances (dikaioma) of divine service" -- ceremonial context
    • Rev 15:4, 19:8 "the righteousness (dikaioma) of saints"

    G1378: dogma (δόγμα)¶

    • Transliteration: dogma
    • Pronunciation: dog-mah
    • Part of Speech: neuter noun
    • BLB Count: 5
    • Definition: from dokeo; a law (civil, ceremonial or ecclesiastical) -- decree, ordinance
    • Total KJV occurrences: 5
    • Primary translations: "decrees" (2x, 40%), "a decree" (1x), "ordinances" (1x), "of ordinances" (1x)
    • KEY USES:
    • Luk 2:1 "a decree (dogma) from Caesar Augustus"
    • Act 16:4 "the decrees (dogma) that were ordained of the apostles"
    • Act 17:7 "the decrees (dogma) of Caesar"
    • Eph 2:15 "the law of commandments contained in ordinances (dogma)" -- CRITICAL: what was abolished
    • Col 2:14 "the handwriting of ordinances (dogma) that was against us" -- CRITICAL: what was nailed to the cross
    • NOTE: dogma is NEVER used of the Ten Commandments/Decalogue; it refers to decrees, regulations

    G3037: lithos (λίθος)¶

    • Transliteration: lithos
    • Pronunciation: lee-thos
    • Part of Speech: masculine noun
    • BLB Count: 60
    • Definition: apparently a primary word; a stone (literally or figuratively)
    • Total KJV occurrences: 53
    • Primary translations: "stone" (15x, 28.3%), "stones" (12x, 22.6%), "a stone" (7x, 13.2%)
    • KEY USE: 2Co 3:7 "the ministration of death, written and engraven in stones (lithos)"
    • Also: 2Co 3:3 "not in tables of stone, but in fleshy tables of the heart"

    G5046: teleios (τέλειος)¶

    • Transliteration: teleios
    • Pronunciation: tel-i-os
    • Part of Speech: adjective
    • BLB Count: 19
    • Definition: from telos; complete (in various applications of labor, growth, mental and moral character, etc.); neuter as noun, completeness -- of full age, man, perfect
    • Total KJV occurrences: 12
    • Primary translations: "perfect" (6x, 50%), "are perfect" (1x), "is perfect" (1x)
    • KEY USES:
    • Mat 5:48 "Be ye therefore perfect (teleios), even as your Father which is in heaven is perfect (teleios)"
    • Jas 1:25 "whoso looketh into the perfect (teleios) law of liberty" -- KEY: law described as perfect
    • Jas 1:4 "let patience have her perfect (teleios) work"
    • Jas 1:17 "Every good gift and every perfect (teleios) gift"
    • Rom 12:2 "that good, and acceptable, and perfect (teleios), will of God"

    H5703: ad (עַד)¶

    • Transliteration: ad
    • Pronunciation: ad
    • Part of Speech: masculine noun
    • BLB Count: 49
    • Definition: from adah (to advance); properly, a (peremptory) terminus, i.e. (by implication) duration, in the sense of advance or perpetuity -- eternity, ever(-lasting, -more), old, perpetually, world without end
    • Total KJV occurrences: 51
    • Primary translations: "for ever" (25x, 49%), "and ever" (14x, 27.5%), "for" (3x), "everlasting" (2x)
    • KEY USES:
    • Exo 15:18 "The LORD shall reign for ever and ever (ad)"
    • Psa 9:6 "destructions are come to a perpetual (ad) end"
