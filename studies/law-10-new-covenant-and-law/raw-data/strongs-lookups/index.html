    Strong's Lookups Raw Data¶

    Hebrew Words¶

    H1285 -- berith (covenant)¶

    • Transliteration: berit
    • POS: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 284 occurrences
    • Definition: from H1254 (in the sense of cutting); a compact (because made by passing between pieces of flesh): confederacy, covenant, league
    • Key verses: Gen 6:18; 9:9-17; 15:18; 17:1-21; Exo 19:5; 24:7-8; 34:10,28; Deut 4:13; 5:2-3; 29:1; Jer 31:31-33; 32:40; Eze 16:60-62; 34:25; 37:26

    H3772 -- karath (cut)¶

    • Transliteration: karath
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: 288 occurrences
    • Definition: a primitive root; to cut (off, down or asunder); specifically, to covenant (i.e. make an alliance or bargain, originally by cutting flesh and passing between the pieces): be chewed, covenant, cut (down, off), destroy, make a league
    • Key verses: Gen 15:18; 21:27; Exo 24:8; 34:10,27; Deut 5:2-3; Jer 31:31-33; 32:40; 34:18; Eze 34:25; 37:26

    H3820 -- leb (heart)¶

    • Transliteration: leb
    • POS: masculine noun
    • BLB Count: 593 occurrences
    • Definition: the heart; used figuratively for the feelings, the will and even the intellect; likewise for the centre of anything: heart, mind, understanding, will, consent
    • Key verses: Deut 6:5-6; 30:6; Psa 37:31; 51:10; Jer 24:7; 31:33; Eze 11:19; 36:26

    H3212 -- yalak (walk)¶

    • Transliteration: yalak
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: ~500 occurrences
    • Definition: a primitive root; to walk (literally or figuratively); causatively, to carry: go, walk, come, depart, lead, march, prosper
    • Key verses: Lev 26:3; Deut 8:6; 10:12; 11:22; 1 Ki 2:4; 6:12; Eze 11:20; 20:19; 36:27; 37:24

    H8085 -- shama (hear/obey)¶

    • Transliteration: shama
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: ~1100 occurrences
    • Definition: a primitive root; to hear intelligently (often with implication of attention, obedience): attentively, call together, consent, consider, give ear, hear, listen, obedient, obey, perceive, regard, understand
    • Key verses: Exo 15:26; Deut 4:30; 6:4; 30:10; 1 Sam 15:22; Jer 7:23; 11:3-4; 31:18

    H2710 -- chaqaq (engrave/decree)¶

    • Transliteration: chaqaq
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: 19 occurrences
    • Definition: a primitive root; properly, to hack, i.e. engrave; by implication, to enact (laws being cut in stone or metal tablets): appoint, decree, governor, grave, lawgiver, note, pourtray, print, set
    • Key verses: Gen 49:10; Num 21:18; Deut 33:21; Judg 5:9,14; Prov 8:15,27,29; Isa 10:1; 22:16; 30:8; 33:22; 49:16

    H6662 -- tsaddiq (righteous)¶

    • Transliteration: tsaddiq
    • POS: adjective
    • BLB Count: 206 occurrences
    • Definition: from H6663; just: just, lawful, righteous (man)
    • Key verses: Gen 6:9; 7:1; 18:23-26; Psa 1:6; 7:10-12; 11:5,7; 119:137; 145:17; Prov 4:18; Isa 53:11; Hab 2:4

    H6666 -- tsedaqah (righteousness)¶

    • Transliteration: tsedaqah
    • POS: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 157 occurrences
    • Definition: from H6663; rightness (abstractly), subjectively (rectitude), objectively (justice), morally (virtue): justice, moderately, righteous (act, -ly, -ness)
    • Key verses: Gen 15:6; Deut 6:25; 33:21; Psa 24:6; 71:2,15-16,19; 119:40,142; Isa 5:7; 45:8; 54:17; Dan 9:7,16

    Greek Words¶

    G1449 -- engrapho (inscribe)¶

    • Transliteration: engrapho
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: 2 occurrences ONLY
    • Definition: from G1722 + G1125; to engrave, inscribe: write (in)
    • Occurrences: Luke 10:20; 2 Corinthians 3:2-3
    • NOTE: This is extremely rare (only 2 NT occurrences). In 2 Cor 3:3, it describes being "written/inscribed" on hearts. Different from G1924 (epigrapho) used in Heb 8:10; 10:16.

    G4102 -- pistis (faith)¶

    • Transliteration: pistis
    • POS: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 244 occurrences
    • Definition: from G3982; persuasion, credence; moral conviction: assurance, belief, faith, fidelity
    • Key verses: Rom 1:5,17; 3:22,25,28,31; 4:5,16; 5:1; Gal 2:16; 3:2,11,22-25; Eph 2:8; Heb 11:1-39; Rev 14:12

    G1785 -- entole (commandment)¶

    • Transliteration: entole
    • POS: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 71 occurrences
    • Definition: from G1781; injunction, i.e. an authoritative prescription: commandment, precept
    • Key verses: Mat 5:19; 15:3; 19:17; 22:36,38; John 13:34; 14:15,21; 15:10,12; Rom 7:8-13; 13:9; 1 Cor 7:19; 1 John 2:3,7; 3:22-24; 5:2-3; Rev 12:17; 14:12; 22:14

    G5218 -- hupakoe (obedience)¶

    • Transliteration: hupakoe
    • POS: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 15 occurrences
    • Definition: from G5219; attentive hearkening, compliance or submission: obedience, obey
    • Key verses: Rom 1:5; 5:19; 6:16; 15:18; 16:19,26; 2 Cor 7:15; 10:5-6; Phm 1:21; Heb 5:8; 1 Pet 1:2,14,22

    G1345 -- dikaioma (righteous requirement/ordinance)¶

    • Transliteration: dikaioma
    • POS: neuter noun
    • BLB Count: 10 occurrences
    • Definition: from G1344; an equitable deed; by implication, a statute or decision: judgment, justification, ordinance, righteousness
    • Key verses: Deut 30:16 (LXX); Luke 1:6; Rom 1:32; 2:26; 5:16,18; 8:4; Heb 7:16; 9:1,10; Rev 15:4; 19:8

    G1343 -- dikaiosyne (righteousness)¶

    • Transliteration: dikaiosyne
    • POS: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 92 occurrences
    • Definition: from G1342; equity (of character or act); especially Christian justification: righteousness
    • Key verses: Mat 5:6,10,20; 6:33; Rom 1:17; 3:5,21-22,25; 4:3-13; 5:17,21; 8:10; 9:30; 10:3-6; 2 Cor 3:9; Gal 2:21; 3:6,21; Phil 3:9

    G1397 -- douleia (bondage/slavery)¶

    • Transliteration: douleia
    • POS: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 5 occurrences
    • Definition: from G1398; slavery (ceremonially or figuratively): bondage
    • Occurrences: Rom 8:15,21; Gal 4:24; 5:1; Heb 2:15
    • NOTE: Contrasts old covenant bondage (Gal 4:24) with new covenant freedom

    G1450 -- enguos (guarantor/surety)¶

    • Transliteration: enguos
    • POS: adjective
    • BLB Count: 1 occurrence ONLY
    • Definition: pledged, i.e. a bondsman: surety
    • Occurrence: Hebrews 7:22 -- "By so much was Jesus made a surety of a better testament"
    • NOTE: Hapax legomenon (appears only once in NT). Jesus is the GUARANTOR of the better covenant.

    G570 -- apistia (unbelief)¶

    • Transliteration: apistia
    • POS: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 12 occurrences
    • Definition: from G571; faithlessness, disbelief: unbelief
    • Key verses: Mat 13:58; 17:20; Mark 6:6; 9:24; 16:14; Rom 3:3; 4:20; 11:20,23; 1 Tim 1:13; Heb 3:12,19

    G802 -- asunthetos (covenant-breaking)¶

    • Transliteration: asunthetos
    • POS: adjective
    • BLB Count: 1 occurrence in NT
    • Definition: properly, not agreed, treacherous to compacts: covenant-breaker
    • NT Occurrence: Romans 1:31
    • LXX connections: 1 Chr 9:1; Neh 1:8; Psa 72:15; 73:15; Jer 3:7,8,11
    • NOTE: Links covenant-breaking to the catalogue of sins in Rom 1

    G3980 -- peitharcheo (obey authority)¶

    • Transliteration: peitharcheo
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: 4 occurrences
    • Definition: from a compound of G3982 + G757; to be persuaded by a ruler, obey a magistrate: hearken, obey
    • Occurrences: Acts 5:29,32; 27:21; Titus 3:1

    G1384 -- dokimos (approved/tested)¶

    • Transliteration: dokimos
    • POS: adjective
    • BLB Count: 7 occurrences
    • Definition: properly, acceptable (current after assayal), approved: approved, tried
    • Key verses: Rom 14:18; 16:10; 1 Cor 11:19; 2 Cor 10:18; 13:7; 2 Tim 2:15; Jas 1:12
