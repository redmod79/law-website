    Raw Strong's Lookup Data¶

    H1285 berith -- "covenant"¶

    • 317 occurrences, 45 unique translations
    • Top: "of the covenant" (60), "my covenant" (45), "covenant" (40), "a covenant" (40), "the covenant" (27)
    • Lexicon: feminine noun; from H1254 (cutting); a compact (made by passing between pieces of flesh)
    • Definition: confederacy, (con-)feder(-ate), covenant, league

    H3772 karath -- "to cut"¶

    • 358 occurrences, 156 unique translations
    • Top: "shall be cut off" (30), "made" (18), "cut off" (12), "he made" (8)
    • Lexicon: verb; primitive root; to cut (off, down or asunder); specifically, to covenant (make an alliance by cutting flesh)
    • Definition: be chewed, be con-(feder-)ate, covenant, cut (down, off), destroy, fail, feller, be freed, hew (down), make a league (covenant)

    G1242 diatheke -- "covenant/testament"¶

    • 33 occurrences, 5 unique translations
    • "covenant" (15, 45.5%), "testament" (11, 33.3%), "covenants" (3), "the covenant" (2), "a testament" (2)
    • Lexicon: feminine noun; from G1303; properly, a disposition, i.e. a contract (especially a devisory will)
    • Used in: Heb 7:22; 8:6,8,8,9,9,10; 9:4,15,15,15,16,16,18,18,20; 10:16,29; 12:24; 13:20

    G3316 mesites -- "mediator"¶

    • 6 occurrences, 4 unique translations
    • "the mediator" (3), "of a mediator" (1), "a mediator" (1), "mediator" (1)
    • Lexicon: masculine noun; from G3319; a go-between, i.e. an internunciator or reconciler
    • All occurrences: Gal 3:19,20; 1Ti 2:5; Heb 8:6; 9:15; 12:24

    G1450 engyos -- "surety"¶

    • 1 occurrence: Heb 7:22 -- "Jesus made a surety of a better testament"
    • Lexicon: adjective; pledged, i.e. a bondsman

    G3822 palaioo -- "to make old"¶

    • 6 occurrences (4 BLB); "old" (2), "which wax" (1), "shall wax old" (1), "he hath made" (1), "that which decayeth" (1)
    • Lexicon: verb; from G3820; to make (passively, become) worn out, or declare obsolete
    • Key: Heb 8:13 (pepalaioken = perfect active "has made old"; palaioumenon = present passive "being made old")

    G854 aphanismos -- "vanishing"¶

    • 1 occurrence: Heb 8:13 -- "ready to vanish away"
    • Lexicon: masculine noun; from G853; disappearance, i.e. (figuratively) abrogation

    G2647 kataluo -- "to destroy"¶

    • 20 occurrences (17 BLB); "to destroy" (3), "be thrown down" (3), "destroyest" (2)
    • Lexicon: verb; from G2596+G3089; to loosen down (disintegrate), demolish
    • Key: Mat 5:17 -- "not come to destroy (katalusai) the law"

    G2476 histemi -- "to stand/establish"¶

    • 138 occurrences (158 BLB); "stood" (30), "standing" (19), "stand" (15)
    • Lexicon: verb; to stand (transitively or intransitively); establish, appoint, present, set up
    • Key: Rom 3:31 -- "we establish (histanomen) the law"

    H3820 leb -- "heart"¶

    • 649 occurrences (593 BLB); "heart" (102), "my heart" (39), "his heart" (37)
    • Lexicon: masculine noun; the heart; widely for feelings, will, intellect; centre of anything
    • Key: Jer 31:33 -- "write it in their hearts (libbam)"

    H3824 lebab -- "heart/inner man"¶

    • 267 occurrences (252 BLB); "thine heart" (28), "heart" (24), "his heart" (22)
    • Lexicon: masculine noun; the heart (as the most interior organ)
    • Key: Deu 30:6 -- "circumcise thine heart (lebab)"

    G2588 kardia -- "heart"¶

    • 142 occurrences (160 BLB); "heart" (69), "hearts" (49)
    • Lexicon: feminine noun; the heart, i.e. thoughts or feelings (mind)
    • Key: Heb 8:10; 10:16 -- "write them in their hearts (kardias)"

    H7130 qereb -- "inward parts"¶

    • 232 occurrences (227 BLB); "in the midst" (28), "among you" (17), "the inwards" (16)
    • Lexicon: masculine noun; the nearest part, centre; among, bowels, heart, inward, midst
    • Key: Jer 31:33 -- "in their inward parts (b'qirbam)"

    G1449 engrapho -- "to inscribe"¶

    • 2 occurrences; "written" (2)
    • Lexicon: verb; from G1722+G1125; to "engrave", inscribe; write (in)
    • All: Luk 10:20; 2Cor 3:2,3

    H5137 nazah -- "to sprinkle"¶

    • 28 occurrences (24 BLB); "and sprinkle" (8), "he shall sprinkle" (3)
    • Lexicon: verb; to spirt, besprinkle (especially in expiation)

    G4473 rhantismos -- "sprinkling"¶

    • 2 occurrences; "of sprinkling" (1), "sprinkling" (1)
    • Lexicon: masculine noun; aspersion (ceremonially or figuratively)
    • All: Heb 12:24; 1Pe 1:2

    G129 haima -- "blood"¶

    • 89 occurrences (99 BLB); "blood" (69), "of blood" (8), "the blood" (5)
    • Lexicon: neuter noun; blood, literally or figuratively

    G1860 epangelia -- "promise"¶

    • 51 occurrences (53 BLB); "promise" (26), "promises" (11), "of promise" (6)
    • Lexicon: feminine noun; an announcement, divine assurance of good
    • Key: Heb 8:6 -- "established upon better promises"

    G2909 kreitton -- "better"¶

    • 17 occurrences (18 BLB); "better" (6), "of a better" (3), "a better" (3)
    • Lexicon: adjective; comparative of G2904; stronger, better
    • Key: Heb 7:22; 8:6 -- "better covenant", "better promises"
