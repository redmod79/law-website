# Topics Research

## Question
Is the Sabbath moral or ceremonial? Apply all criteria from studies 01-05 and 24 systematically: (1) Creation origin, (2) Decalogue membership, (3) God's voice/finger/stone, (4) Lev 23:37-38 distinction, (5) Typological function, (6) Heb 4:9 sabbatismos, (7) Universal scope. Investigate Isaiah 56:1-8 (foreigners/Gentiles keeping Sabbath). Address the accountability principle.
