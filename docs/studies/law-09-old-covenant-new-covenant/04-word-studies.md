# Word Studies

## Covenant Terms

### berith (H1285) -- "covenant"
**Original:** בְּרִית
**Transliteration:** b'riyth
**Pronunciation:** ber-eeth
**Part of Speech:** feminine noun
**Occurrences:** 317 total (284 BLB count)

**Definition:** From [H1254](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/h1254/kjv/wlc/0-1/){:target="_blank"} (in the sense of cutting [like H1262]); a compact (because made by passing between pieces of flesh): -- confederacy, (con-)feder(-ate), covenant, league.

**Translation Distribution:**
- "of the covenant" -- 60 (18.9%)
- "my covenant" -- 45 (14.2%)
- "covenant" -- 40 (12.6%)
- "a covenant" -- 40 (12.6%)
- "the covenant" -- 27 (8.5%)
- "his covenant" -- 14 (4.4%)
- "a league" -- 11 (3.5%)

**Key Observations:**
- Used for both the Sinai covenant (Exo 19:5; 24:7-8; 34:28; Deu 4:13; 5:2-3; 9:9,11,15)
- Used for the new covenant (Jer 31:31,32,33)
- Used for Abrahamic covenant (Gen 15:18; 17:2ff)
- Used for covenant of peace (Num 25:12; Eze 34:25; 37:26)
- Etymology: "cutting" -- related to the idiom "to cut a covenant" (karath berith)

### karath (H3772) -- "to cut"
**Original:** כָּרַת
**Transliteration:** karath
**Pronunciation:** kaw-rath
**Part of Speech:** verb
**Occurrences:** 358 total (288 BLB count)

**Definition:** A primitive root; to cut (off, down or asunder); by implication, to destroy or consume; specifically, to covenant (i.e. make an alliance or bargain, originally by cutting flesh and passing between the pieces): -- be chewed, be con-(feder-)ate, covenant, cut (down, off), destroy, fail, feller, be freed, hew (down), make a league (covenant), lose, perish, utterly, want.

**Key Translation Data:**
- "shall be cut off" -- 30 (8.4%)
- "made" [a covenant] -- 18 (5.0%)
- "cut off" -- 8-12 (various forms)
- "he made" [a covenant] -- 8 (2.2%)

**Key Covenant Usage:**
- Jer 31:31: "I will make (karath) a new covenant" -- lit. "I will cut a new covenant"
- Jer 31:32: "the covenant that I made (karath) with their fathers"
- Jer 31:33: "the covenant that I will make (karath) with the house of Israel"
- Exo 24:8: "the covenant, which the LORD hath made (karath) with you"
- Exo 34:27: "I have made (karath) a covenant with thee and with Israel"

## Covenant Transition Terms

### palaioo (G3822) -- "to make old/obsolete"
**Original:** παλαιόω
**Transliteration:** palaioo
**Pronunciation:** pal-ah-yo-o
**Part of Speech:** verb
**Occurrences:** 6 total (4 BLB count)

**Definition:** From [G3820](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g3820/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"}; to make (passively, become) worn out, or declare obsolete: -- decay, make (wax) old.

**Translation Distribution:**
- "old" -- 2
- "which wax" -- 1
- "shall wax old" -- 1
- "he hath made" -- 1
- "that which decayeth" -- 1

**Key Usage:**
- Heb 8:13 -- "he hath made the first old (pepalaioken -- Perfect Active Indicative)" and "that which decayeth (palaioumenon -- Present Passive Participle) and waxeth old"
- Heb 1:11 -- "they all shall wax old as doth a garment"
- Luk 12:33 -- "bags which wax not old"

**Greek Grammar (from Heb 8:13 parsing):**
- πεπαλαίωκεν (pepalaioken) = Perfect Active Indicative 3rd Singular -- "he has made old" (completed action with ongoing result)
- παλαιούμενον (palaioumenon) = Present Passive Participle Nominative Neuter -- "the thing being made old" (ongoing process)

## Heart/Internalization Terms

### leb (H3820) -- "heart"
**Original:** לֵב
**Transliteration:** leb
**Pronunciation:** labe
**Part of Speech:** masculine noun
**Occurrences:** 649 total (593 BLB count)

**Definition:** A form of [H3824](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/h3824/kjv/wlc/0-1/){:target="_blank"}; the heart; also used (figuratively) very widely for the feelings, the will and even the intellect; likewise for the centre of anything.

## Blood/Ratification Terms

### nazah (H5137) -- "to sprinkle"
**Original:** נָזָה
**Transliteration:** nazah
**Pronunciation:** naw-zaw
**Part of Speech:** verb
**Occurrences:** 28 total (24 BLB count)

**Definition:** A primitive root; to spirt, i.e. besprinkle (especially in expiation): -- sprinkle.

**Key Usage:**
- Exo 24:6 -- Moses "sprinkled" half the blood on the altar (covenant ratification)

- Lev 16:14-19 -- Day of Atonement sprinkling
- Isa 52:15 -- "so shall he sprinkle many nations" (Messianic)
