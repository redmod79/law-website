# Raw Strong's Lookups — Law 08

## [G2673](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g2673/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} katargeo
- 32 total KJV occurrences, 30 unique translations
- Key translations: "shall destroy" (2), "is done away" (2), plus 28 other unique renderings
- Lexicon: "to be (render) entirely idle (useless)"
- Verses: Luk 13:7; Rom 3:3,31; 4:14; 6:6; 7:2,6; 1 Cor 1:28; 2:6; 6:13; 13:8,10,11; 15:24,26; 2 Cor 3:7,11,13,14; Gal 3:17; 5:4,11; Eph 2:15; 2 Th 2:8; 2 Tim 1:10; Heb 2:14

## [G5498](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g5498/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} cheirographon

- Translation: "the handwriting"
- Lexicon: "something hand-written" (cheir + grapho)

## [G1345](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1345/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} dikaioma

## [G2647](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g2647/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} kataluo

- Key verse: Mat 5:17 — "Think not that I am come to destroy (katalusai) the law"

## [G1805](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1805/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} exagorazo

- Translations: "hath redeemed" (1), "redeem" (1), "Redeeming" (1), "redeeming" (1)
- Lexicon: "to buy up, i.e. ransom; figuratively, to rescue from loss"
- Key verse: Gal 3:13 — "Christ hath redeemed us from the curse of the law"

## [G1944](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1944/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} epikataratos

- Lexicon: "imprecated, i.e. execrable"
- Verses: Gal 3:10,13
- LXX: Deu 21:23; 27:26

## [G3551](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g3551/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} nomos
- 169 total KJV occurrences, 11 unique translations
- Dominant: "law" (95), "the law" (49), "of the law" (14)
- Lexicon: "anything established, anything received by usage, a custom, a law"
- Most frequent law term in NT; context determines which law

## [G1785](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1785/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} entole
- 43 total KJV occurrences, 8 unique translations
- Dominant: "commandment" (23), "commandments" (12)
- Lexicon: "injunction, i.e. an authoritative prescription"
- Key: used for moral commandments (1 Cor 7:19; Rev 12:17; 14:12) but also for the Levitical commandment (Heb 7:5,16,18)

## [G4716](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g4716/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} stauros
- 22 total KJV occurrences, 3 unique translations
- Dominant: "cross" (20)
- Lexicon: "a stake or post (as set upright)"
- Key: Col 2:14 — "nailing it to his cross"

## [H8451](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/h8451/kjv/wlc/0-1/){:target="_blank"} torah
- 244 total KJV occurrences, 47 unique translations
- Dominant: "the law" (60), "of the law" (28), "law" (24), "thy law" (20)
- Lexicon: "a precept or statute, especially the Decalogue or Pentateuch"
- Note: root yarah = "to instruct, direct"

## [H4941](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/h4941/kjv/wlc/0-1/){:target="_blank"} mishpat
- 448 total KJV occurrences, 133 unique translations
- Dominant: "judgment" (69), "my judgments" (23), "of judgment" (21)
- Lexicon: "a verdict pronounced judicially, a sentence or formal decree"
- Widest semantic range of any Hebrew law term
