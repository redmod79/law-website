# Topics Research

## Question
In Matthew 5:17-20, Jesus declares He came "not to destroy, but to fulfil" the law, states "till heaven and earth pass, one jot or one tittle shall in no wise pass from the law," warns that breaking "one of these least commandments" affects kingdom status, and demands righteousness "exceeding the scribes and Pharisees." What do these four verses actually say about the continuity, authority, and scope of the law?
