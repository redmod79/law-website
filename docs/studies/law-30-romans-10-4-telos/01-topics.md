# Topics Research

## Question
What does telos (G5056) mean in Romans 10:4? Termination, goal/purpose, or fulfillment?

**Source:** Nave's Topical Bible

**General scriptures:** PSA 19:7-9; 119:1-8; PRO 28:4,5; MAT 22:21; LUK 16:17; 20:22-25; ROM 2:14,15; 7:7,12,14; 13:10; 1TI 1:5,8-10; JAS 1:25; 1JN 3:4; 5:3

**Note for analysis:** Nave's classifies Rom 10:4 under "TEMPORARY" -- this reflects one interpretive tradition. The analysis must evaluate whether this classification is warranted by the text.
