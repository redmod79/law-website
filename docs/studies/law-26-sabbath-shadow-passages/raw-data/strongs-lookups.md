# Strong's Lookups -- Raw Data

## Greek Words

### [G4521](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g4521/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} sabbaton
- Transliteration: sab'-bat-on
- POS: neuter noun
- Count: 68
- Definition: Of Hebrew origin (H7676); the Sabbath (i.e. Shabbath), or day of weekly repose
- KJV translations: sabbath (19x), sabbath day (13x), sabbath days (7x), of the week (4x), the sabbath day (3x), week (3x), of the sabbath (2x), others (4x)
- Col 2:16 parsing: Gen Pl N (sabbaton)

### [G4520](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g4520/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} sabbatismos
- Transliteration: sab-bat-is-mos'
- POS: masculine noun
- Count: 1 (hapax legomenon)
- Definition: From a derivative of [G4521](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g4521/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"}; a "sabbatism," i.e. the repose of Christianity
- Only in Heb 4:9

### [G4639](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g4639/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} skia
- Transliteration: skee'-ah
- POS: feminine noun
- Count: 7
- Definition: shade or a shadow (literally or figuratively)
- Theological uses: Col 2:17, Heb 8:5, Heb 10:1 -- all applied to ceremonial system
- Literal uses: Mat 4:16, Mrk 4:32, Luk 1:79, Acts 5:15

### [G5498](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g5498/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} cheirographon
- Transliteration: khi-rog'-raf-on
- POS: neuter noun
- Count: 1 (hapax legomenon)
- Definition: compound of cheir (hand) + grapho (write) = hand-written document
- Only in Col 2:14

### [G1378](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1378/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} dogma
- Transliteration: dog'-mah
- POS: neuter noun
- Count: 5
- Definition: a law (civil, ceremonial, or ecclesiastical)
- Occurrences: Luk 2:1 (Caesar's decree), Acts 16:4 (apostolic decrees), Acts 17:7 (Caesar's decrees), Eph 2:15 (ordinances abolished), Col 2:14 (ordinances nailed)
- Never used for the Decalogue

### [G1379](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1379/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} dogmatizo
- Transliteration: dog-mat-id'-zo
- POS: verb
- Count: 1 (NT hapax)
- Definition: to prescribe by statute, to submit to ceremonial rules
- Only in Col 2:20

### [G3561](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g3561/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} noumenia
- Transliteration: noo-may-nee'-ah
- POS: feminine noun
- Count: 1 (NT hapax)
- Definition: the festival of new moon
- Only in Col 2:16

### [G1859](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1859/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} heorte
- Transliteration: heh-or-tay'
- POS: feminine noun
- Count: 27
- Definition: a festival: feast, holyday
- Used for Jewish feasts throughout NT; Col 2:16 uses Gen Sg F (heortes)

### [G1253](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1253/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} diakrisis
- Transliteration: dee-ak'-ree-sis
- POS: feminine noun
- Count: 3
- Definition: judicial estimation: discerning, disputation
- Rom 14:1 (doubtful disputations), 1 Cor 12:10 (discerning of spirits), Heb 5:14 (discern good/evil)

### [G4983](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g4983/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} soma

- Count: 146

## Hebrew Words

### [H7676](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/h7676/kjv/wlc/0-1/){:target="_blank"} shabbath
- Transliteration: shab-bawth'
- POS: masc/fem noun
- Count: 108
- Definition: intermission, the Sabbath
- Used for both weekly Sabbath and in ceremonial triad

### [H7677](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/h7677/kjv/wlc/0-1/){:target="_blank"} shabbathown
- Transliteration: shab-baw-thone'
- POS: masculine noun
- Count: 11
- Definition: a sabbatism or special holiday: rest, sabbath
- shabbath shabbathown = weekly Sabbath (Lev 23:3), Day of Atonement (Lev 23:32)
- shabbathown alone = Trumpets (Lev 23:24), Tabernacles (Lev 23:39)

### [H4150](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/h4150/kjv/wlc/0-1/){:target="_blank"} moed
- Transliteration: mo-ade'
- POS: masculine noun
- Count: 223
- Definition: appointment, fixed time, festival, assembly, place of meeting
- Used for annual feasts in Lev 23:2,4,37,44; Gen 1:14 (seasons)
- Weekly Sabbath is NEVER called moed

### [H2282](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/h2282/kjv/wlc/0-1/){:target="_blank"} chag
- Transliteration: khag
- POS: masculine noun
- Count: 62
- Definition: festival, pilgrimage feast
- Used for 3 pilgrimage feasts; weekly Sabbath NEVER called chag

### [H905](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/h905/kjv/wlc/0-1/){:target="_blank"} bad (millibad)
- Transliteration: bad
- POS: masculine noun (used prepositionally)
- Count: 56
- Definition: separation; as adverb: apart, only, besides
- Lev 23:38: fourfold millibad separating weekly sabbaths from feasts

### [H2142](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/h2142/kjv/wlc/0-1/){:target="_blank"} zakar
- Transliteration: zaw-kar'
- POS: verb
- Count: 233
- Definition: to mark, remember, mention, be male
- Exo 20:8: "Remember the sabbath day" -- memorial verb pointing backward

### [H2320](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/h2320/kjv/wlc/0-1/){:target="_blank"} chodesh
- Transliteration: kho'-desh
- POS: masculine noun
- Count: 276
- Definition: the new moon; by implication a month
- In ceremonial triad: 1 Chr 23:31, 2 Chr 31:3, Eze 45:17, Hos 2:11
