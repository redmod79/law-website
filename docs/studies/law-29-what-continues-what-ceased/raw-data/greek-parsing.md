Key observations:
- men...de contrast: "here [Levitical system] on one hand...there [Melchizedek] on the other"
- Present tense lambanoousin for Levitical tithe-receiving; present tense ze for Melchizedek living
- apothneskontes = present active participle "dying ones" -- mortal Levitical priests
- martyroumenos hoti ze = "being testified that he lives" -- Scripture's witness about Melchizedek
