# Nave's Topical Dictionary Raw Data

## COVENANT
- Sacred (Jos 9:18-21; Gal 3:15); Binding (Jos 9:18-20; Jer 34:8-21; Eze 17:14-18; Gal 3:15)
- The Mosaic law called a covenant (Exo 34:28)
- OF GOD WITH MEN: Confirmed with oath (Gen 22:16; Psa 89:35; Heb 6:13,17,18); Binding (Lev 26; Gal 3:15); Everlasting (Gen 8:20-22; 9:1-17; Psa 105:8,10; Isa 54:10; 61:8); God faithful to (Lev 26:44,45; Deut 4:31; 7:8,9; Jdg 2:1; 1 Ki 8:23; Psa 105:8-11; 106:45; 111:5; Mic 7:20)
- Repudiated by God on account of Jews' idolatry (Jer 44:26,27; Heb 8:9)
- Broken by the Jews (Jer 22:9; Eze 16:59; Heb 8:9)
- Of the Ten Commandments (Exo 34:28; Deut 5:2,3; 9:9)
- THE SECOND COVENANT: Jer 31:31-34; Heb 8:4-13; 12:18-24; 13:20
