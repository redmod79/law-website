### [H3772](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/h3772/kjv/wlc/0-1/){:target="_blank"} -- karath (cut)
- **Transliteration:** karath
- **POS:** verb
- **BLB Count:** 288 occurrences
- **Definition:** a primitive root; to cut (off, down or asunder); specifically, to covenant (i.e. make an alliance or bargain, originally by cutting flesh and passing between the pieces): be chewed, covenant, cut (down, off), destroy, make a league
- **Key verses:** Gen 15:18; 21:27; Exo 24:8; 34:10,27; Deut 5:2-3; Jer 31:31-33; 32:40; 34:18; Eze 34:25; 37:26
