### SYNAGOGUE
**Key entries from Nave's:** ACT 13:43; ACT 9:2,20; ACT 14:1; LUK 4:15-33; MRK 1:39; MRK 6:2
- The synagogue was the regular place for Sabbath worship in apostolic times
- Paul's pattern: entering the synagogue on the Sabbath (Acts 13:14; 17:1-2; 18:4)

### COVENANT
**Key verse references:** JOS 9:18-21; GAL 3:15; JER 31:31-34; HEB 8:4-13
- Sabbath called "a perpetual covenant" (berit olam) in Exo 31:16
- New covenant writes the SAME law on hearts (Jer 31:33; Heb 8:10)

### HOLINESS
**Key verse references:** GEN 17:1; EXO 19:6; LEV 19:2; HEB 4:3,9
- God sanctified the seventh day at creation (Gen 2:3)
- The Sabbath called "the holy of the LORD" (Isa 58:13)

## Related Topics Discovered

### SABBATIC YEAR
- EXO 23:9-11; LEV 25; DEU 15:9; 31:10
- Uses same vocabulary as weekly Sabbath (shabbath shabbathon, Lev 25:4)
- Distinct from the weekly Sabbath in temporal scope

### JUDAISM
- ACT 15:1; 21:20-25; GAL 3; 4; 5; 6
- Relevant to the objection that Sabbath observance is "Judaizing"
- The Jerusalem Council addressed ceremonial requirements, not the Decalogue
